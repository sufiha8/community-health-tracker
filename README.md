# Community Health Tracker

## Project Overview
Community Health Tracker is a web-based platform designed to help individuals and communities manage and monitor health-related information digitally. The system allows users to maintain their personal health logs and download health reports when required for hospital verification or medical documentation.

The platform also includes an administrative system where community administrators can post important announcements such as vaccination drives, health camps, and public health alerts. Users can interact with these announcements by posting comments or queries directly to the administrator.

The system also includes a **real-time notification mechanism** where both users and administrators receive alerts for new announcements, comments, and important community updates.

Additionally, users can send **emergency health alerts** to the administrator in case of a critical medical situation. This feature enables the admin to quickly review the case and provide immediate support or arrange **financial assistance for medical emergencies** within the community.

Another important feature of the system is the **Admin Health Dashboard**, which provides summarized community health statistics. This dashboard can be used by government health officers during quarterly or yearly surveys to quickly analyze the health condition of a community without manually visiting each household and collecting data.

The goal of the system is to improve communication between communities and administrators while maintaining organized health records for individuals.


## Key Features

### User Features
- User registration and secure login
- Maintain and update personal health records
- Add health log details such as Blood Pressure (BP) and Sugar levels
- View community health announcements posted by the admin
- Comment on announcements to ask questions or clarify doubts
- Receive notifications for:
  - New announcements
  - Comments on posts
  - Community updates
- Download personal health reports for hospital submission
- Track personal health history
- View **Health Trend Panel** that displays trends of health metrics such as **Blood Pressure (BP)** and **Sugar levels**
- Send **Health Emergency Alerts** to the admin for urgent medical situations


### Admin Features
- Admin dashboard to manage community health information
- **Community Health Analytics Dashboard** to show summarized health statistics
- Dashboard can be used during **government health surveys**
- Post announcements related to:
  - Vaccination drives
  - Health camps
  - Community health alerts
- Receive notifications when users comment or raise alerts
- Monitor user health records
- Respond to user queries through announcement comments
- Review emergency alerts raised by users
- Provide **quick financial support or assistance** in case of medical emergencies

## System Workflow

1. Users register and log into the system.
2. Users record and update their personal health data such as BP and sugar levels.
3. The system stores this information securely in the Firebase database.
4. Users can view their **health trend panel**, which shows graphical trends of their health data.
5. The admin posts announcements related to vaccination drives, health camps, and public health updates.
6. Users receive notifications whenever a new announcement is posted.
7. Users can comment on announcements to ask questions or seek clarification.
8. Both admin and users receive notifications for comments and updates.
9. In case of a medical emergency, users can send an **alert request to the admin**.
10. The admin can review the alert and provide **financial or community assistance**.
11. The **Admin Dashboard** aggregates community health data and presents it in graphical form.
12. Government health officers can use this dashboard during surveys to analyze the health status of the community.
